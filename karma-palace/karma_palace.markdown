# Welcome to Karma Palace

**A fast-paced card game where strategy and luck decide who wins!**

## What is Karma Palace?

Karma Palace is a fast-paced and addictive card game where strategy meets luck.

Your goal is simple: be the first to clear your hand while leaving your opponents stuck with the worst cards.

### Key Features

- Simple, intuitive gameplay that’s fun for all skill levels
- Quick matches you can pick up and play anytime
- Smart AI opponents for solo practice
- A stylish, minimalist design that keeps the focus on the game

## How to Get Started
1. **Download the App**: Available on the App Store and Google Play Store. Alternatively, try the [web version](https://karma-palace.web.app/)
2. **Launch the Game**: Open the app and test your karma.
3. **Play**: Challenge yourself or compete with friends.
